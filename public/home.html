Find a rant buddy

Life is tough, and there is nothing like complaining together. The problem? Sometimes people are different! Rant-buddy is here to solve that.

Use the Enneagram System

The Enneagram system is a cool little system developed by some westerners with some hippy-tendencies. Still, we find it intriquing.

Basically, the system consists of 9 types - you can take a test below to determine your type and find someone else in our system that shares your type.

Look up that person that shares your type - you're likely to hate similar things for similar reasons - the PERFECT rant buddy!
